MASSIVE Academy

At MASSIVE Academy we aim to improve education and dream to give every kid a chance to learn how to code and be successful online.

To improve education we focus on both material and method. In terms of material we believe that every day the world around us and the business landscape change faster than education is able to. This results in a growing gap between what students are and should be learning. We try to diminish this gap by teaching students skills that matter. In terms of method, all of our courses follow a skill acquisition model where the students learn by doing as they work towards a clear cut goal that they achieve by completing a project. We don't believe in tests and subjective assessments, but creating things and objective measurements of learning.

We also are working in Tallahassee to bring coding into the school system and learning culture through One Million Lines.

Miles 2 Give

Miles 2 Give is a 501c3 Non-Profit organization that raises funding for sarcoma cancer research by running unprecedented team routes worldwide. We believe we are in a Generation Of A Cure. This 2013 tour our road team ran from San Francisco, CA to Ocean City, MD raising over $103,000 for research. For the next tour we have partnered with Huntsman Cancer Institute to give them direct distribution for research of all funds raised and we will be aiming to raise $500,000 while running 10,000 miles counter-clockwise around the United State of America.
