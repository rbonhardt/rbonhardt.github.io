On Following Your Passion

Personally I’ve always made the most money when I created products, services or events that I loved and wanted to attend. When I reversed the equation and focused on how to make more money, it was always short-lived and not enjoyable. -- Noah Kagan

“Thought is the sculptor who can create the person you want to be… In the long run, men hit only what they aim at.” --Henry David Thoreau, 19th century American naturalist and author
“The world makes way for the man who knows where he is going.” -- Ralph Waldo Emerson
“Give me a stock clerk with a goal and I’ll give you a man who will make history. Give me a man with no goals and I’ll give you a stock clerk.” -- JC Penny
“First say to yourself what you would be; and then do what you have to do.” -- Epictetus
“If one does not know to which port he is sailing, no wind is favorable.” -- Seneca
“You got to be careful if you don’t know where you’re going, because you might not get there.” -- Yogi Berra
